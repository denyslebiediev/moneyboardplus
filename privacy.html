Summary

Money Board Plus is built to collect nothing. There is no account and no sign-in, and everything you enter — your transactions and their notes, categories, budgets, savings goals, and board layout — is currently stored only on your device. The app has no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party code, and it makes no network connections of its own. Because the app never transmits your financial data, we never see it. The app's App Store privacy label is "Data Not Collected."

What We Collect: Nothing

The app does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal or financial information, and it gives us no access to anything you enter — it all stays in the app's private, on-device database. Money Board Plus collects no personal information from anyone, including children, and is not directed at children.

What Stays on Your Device

Everything the app saves lives in its private, on-device database: your transactions (amount, date, and any note you add), categories, budgets, savings goals and contributions, the layout of your board, and your app preferences. The app also keeps simple on-device statistics about which board widgets you use, so it can personalize your board — these never leave your device either.

The app never transmits any of this information, and all of it is removed by "Erase All Data."

How Your Data Is Stored and Protected

Your data is saved locally on your device and is encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection whenever your device is locked. You can turn on an optional Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode lock for the app; this check is performed entirely by iOS through Apple's on-device authentication, and the app never receives your biometric information.

Like most iPhone apps, the app's database can be included in the device backups you control — iCloud Backup or a computer backup. Backups are created and encrypted by iOS under your Apple Account; we have no access to them, and you can exclude Money Board Plus from iCloud Backup in iOS Settings.

Notifications

Money Board Plus can send an optional daily logging reminder and budget alerts. These are local notifications — created, scheduled, and delivered entirely on your device, never through a server — and the app asks your permission before enabling them.

Alert text can include the names you have given to budgets or categories, which may be visible on your lock screen; you can control notification previews in iOS Settings. "Erase All Data" also clears any notifications the app has already delivered.

Third-Party Services

Money Board Plus includes no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising, and sends no data to third parties. The app is distributed through the App Store, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy. Tapping "Privacy Policy" inside the app opens this page in your browser; the page is hosted on GitHub Pages, whose servers may log standard request information (such as your IP address) when serving any website. The App Store badge on our home page is loaded from Apple's servers, which likewise see standard request information when that page loads.

Data Retention & Your Rights

Because the app gives us none of your data, there is nothing for us to retain, share, or delete on our side. You remain in full control on your device: you can permanently erase everything from the app's settings ("Erase All Data"), or remove all app data by deleting Money Board Plus. Since no personal data is ever collected, requests to access or delete your information under laws such as the GDPR and CCPA are satisfied entirely on your own device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. In particular, if a future version of the app adds optional iCloud sync, we will update this policy before that feature ships. The current version will always be available at this URL, and the date below shows when it last changed. Significant changes may also be noted in the app's release notes.
