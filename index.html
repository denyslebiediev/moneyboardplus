Money Board Plus.
Private by design.

Track spending, budgets, and savings goals on a board you design — stored entirely on your iPhone, with nothing collected and nothing shared.

Available for iPhone. Requires iOS 26 or later.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing watching over your shoulder.

Money Board Plus keeps the essentials of personal finance — tracking, budgets, goals, and insights — in one place, working entirely offline.

Your board, your way

Add, remove, and rearrange widgets — spend rings, cash flow, categories, recent transactions, quick add — so what matters to you is always front and center.

Budgets that speak up

Set a monthly limit for any category. The app shows how much you have used and warns you as you approach or pass it.

Savings goals

Set a target, log contributions, and watch progress build toward the things you are saving for.

Charts and insights

Spending by category, trends over time, and month-to-date cash flow — all at a glance.

App Lock

Face ID, Touch ID, or your passcode before the app opens — handled entirely by iOS.

Hide Balances

One tap redacts every amount on screen — budget in public without broadcasting your balance.

Privacy

Your finances stay on your iPhone.

There is no account, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party code. The app makes no network connections of its own — everything you enter is encrypted at rest on your iPhone, and you can erase it in one tap.
