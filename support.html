Getting Started

Money Board Plus tracks money through manual entries. Add a transaction, mark it as income or an expense, enter the amount, and choose a category — your totals and budgets update instantly. There are no bank connections, so you control exactly what is recorded.

Set a monthly budget for any spending category. As you add expenses, Money Board Plus shows how much of each budget you have used and warns you as you approach or pass the limit. Budgets are fixed per month and do not roll over.

Create a goal with a target amount, then log contributions whenever you set money aside. The goal shows your progress toward the target, and you can add a goal widget to your board to keep it in sight.

Yes. The board is the app's home screen and is fully customizable — add, remove, and rearrange widgets such as spend-vs-budget rings, month-to-date cash flow, spending by category, top categories, recent transactions, and quick-add, so the information you care about is front and center.

Privacy & Billing

All of it stays on your device. There is no account, no sign-in, and no server — the app never sends your transactions, budgets, or board anywhere. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Money Board Plus currently has no export or cloud sync, so your data moves with your device backup. When you set up a new iPhone by restoring from an iCloud or computer backup of your old one, your board, transactions, and budgets come across automatically.

Open Settings and turn on App Lock. When it is on, Money Board Plus asks for Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode before opening, so your finances stay private. The check is handled entirely by iOS.

Turn on Hide Balances to redact every amount on screen — handy when budgeting in public. Your numbers stay hidden until you turn it off.

Because your data lives only on your device, open Settings and choose "Erase All Data," or delete the app, to remove everything. Money Board Plus keeps no copy anywhere else.

Yes, it is currently free with no in-app purchases. If paid features are added later, you will always see the price and terms before buying, and purchases go through your Apple Account.
